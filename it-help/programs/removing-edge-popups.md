The image above us just a site you allowed permissions to and it has taken over your edge notifications. This kind of popup will redirect you to malicious site and try to take your data.

### Remove

To clear all data from a website in Microsoft Edge, you can remove all cookies and site data:&#x20;

1. Click the Edge menu (three dots) in the top right corner
2. Click Settings
3. Click Cookies and site permissions on the left side
4. Under Manage and delete cookies and site data, click See all cookies and site data
5. Click Remove all in the new dialogue box

You can also clear your browsing data in Microsoft Edge by following these steps:&#x20;

1. Select Settings and more
2. Select Settings
3. Select Privacy, search, and services
4. Under Delete browsing data, select Clear browsing data now
5. Select Choose what to clear

### Disable notification popups

To turn off pop-ups in Microsoft Edge, you can do the following:&#x20;

1. Select Settings and more in the top right corner of the browser
2. Select Settings
3. Select Cookies and site permissions
4. Under All permissions, select Pop-ups and redirects
5. Turn off the Block (recommended) toggle
