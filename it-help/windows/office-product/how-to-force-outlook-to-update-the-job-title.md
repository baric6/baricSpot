# How to Force Outlook(Classic) to Update the Job Title

#### **1. Force Outlook to Download the Updated Address Book (OAB)**

ðŸ”¹ _For users with Cached Exchange Mode enabled_

1. Open **Outlook**.
2. Click on **File** > **Account Settings** > **Account Settings**.
3. Select your **Exchange account** and click **Change**.
4. Uncheck **Use Cached Exchange Mode**, click **Next**, then **Finish**.
5. Restart Outlook.
6. Re-enable **Cached Exchange Mode**, restart Outlook again.
