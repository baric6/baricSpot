# Outlook Send as or as behalf Email

1. Make a new email
2. Pop out the message to see other settings by  ![](<../../../.gitbook/assets/image (434).png>) in the right corner of message
3. In the top bar go to options&#x20;
4. Check mark the show from field

<figure><img src="../../../.gitbook/assets/image (433).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

5. After it is checked your email and a drop-down arrow should appear next to the send button
6. When you click the down arrow it should show all emails you are delegated to send as or as behalf
7. Select the email and send

\***Note**\*&#x20;

If you don't see any emails you probably are not delegated to emails and contact sysadmin
