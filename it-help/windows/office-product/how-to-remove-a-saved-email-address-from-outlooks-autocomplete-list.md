# How to remove a saved email address from Outlook's autocomplete List

1. Start a new email.
2. Type the beginning of the autocomplete entry you wish to delete.
3. Hover your mouse over the entry in the suggestions until it's highlighted (don't click it).
4. Click the 'X' next to the entry or press the delete key to remove it.\
   Note: This stops the name from showing up in future suggestions.
