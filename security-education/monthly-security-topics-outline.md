# Monthly Security topics Outline

January&#x20;

* New year New habits&#x20;
* Topics: Strong password creation, password manager use, MFA reminders.&#x20;
* **Why:** People are setting resolutions—perfect time to push “security resolutions.”

February&#x20;

* Romance scams and Phishing awareness&#x20;
* Topics: Social engineering, romance scams, email phishing.&#x20;
* **Tie-in:** Valentine’s Day – highlight emotional manipulation in scams.

March&#x20;

* Clean up you digital life&#x20;
* Topics: Removing unused apps, clearing old permissions, software updates.&#x20;
* **Tie-in:** Spring cleaning season.

April&#x20;

* Don’t get fooled by fraud&#x20;
* Topics: Tax scams, identity theft, financial fraud&#x20;
* **Tie-in:** Tax season (and April Fool’s Day!).

May&#x20;

* Securing Devices On the Go&#x20;
* Topics: Public Wi-Fi dangers, VPNs, physical security, lost device protocols.&#x20;
* **Tie-in:** Early summer travel and Memorial Day.

June&#x20;

* Security for the whole family&#x20;
* Topics: Home network security, parental controls, kid-safe browsing.&#x20;
* **Tie-in:** Start of summer break.

July&#x20;

* Defend your inbox&#x20;
* Topics: Phishing emails, spear-phishing, reporting suspicious messages.&#x20;
* **Tie-in:** Independence Day – “Declare independence from phishing.”

August&#x20;

* Device Readiness & Remote work&#x20;
* Topics: Securing work/school laptops, mobile device security, VPN use.&#x20;
* **Tie-in:** Back-to-school season.

September &#x20;

* Trust But Verify&#x20;
* Topics: Insider threats, access control, data loss prevention.&#x20;
* **Tie-in:** End of fiscal year in some orgs, National Insider Threat Awareness Month (U.S.)

October&#x20;

* Cyber Security is everyone's job&#x20;
* Topics: Summary of key topics: phishing, passwords, physical security, reporting.&#x20;
* **Tie-in:** Official Cybersecurity Awareness Month – go big this month!

November&#x20;

* Secure Shopping online&#x20;
* Topics: Safe e-commerce, scam websites, credit card protection, fake deals.&#x20;
* **Tie-in:** Black Friday/Cyber Monday.

December &#x20;

* Staying Safe Over the Holidays&#x20;
* Topics: Holiday scams, travel security, protecting new devices.&#x20;
* **Tie-in:** Holidays, travel, year-end shutdowns.
