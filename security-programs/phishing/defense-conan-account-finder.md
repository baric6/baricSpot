# (defense) Conan account finder

This tool aims at finding the websites where you could have created an account in the past. It does so by logging into your email account and looking for emails that sent you mails. You'll get a complete list of all the domains that sent you emails. The informations related on how to delete accounts are provided by JustDeleteMe.
